# Future Simple

## Main Use
Use it for decisions made in the moment, predictions, offers, and promises.

## Timeline Idea / Visual Meaning
The action is after now, often decided or expressed from the present moment.

```text
PAST ---- NOW ----> FUTURE
             decision / prediction
```

## Structure
- Affirmative: **Subject + will + base verb**
- Negative: **Subject + will not + base verb**
- Question: **Will + subject + base verb?**

## Examples
- I will call you tonight.
- She won't forget.
- Will they join us later?

## Common Markers / Signals
- tomorrow
- tonight
- next week
- I think
- probably
- maybe

## Contrast
Compare with [[Future Going To]] when you want to separate instant decision from plan or intention.

## Common Mistakes
- **I will to call you.** ❌
- **I will call you.** ✅

- **He will goes tomorrow.** ❌
- **He will go tomorrow.** ✅
