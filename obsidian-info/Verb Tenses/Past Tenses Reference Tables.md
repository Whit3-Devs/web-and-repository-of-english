
# Past tenses: affirmative, negative and question forms

## 1) Affirmative form

| Tiempo verbal | Sujeto | Verbo auxiliar | Forma acortada del auxiliar | Verbo principal | Objeto / complemento | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Past Simple | sujeto | — | — | past form | complemento | Subject + verb in past + object | We visited our grandmother. |
| Past Continuous | sujeto | was / were | no common affirmative contraction | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + was/were + verb-ing + object | She was reading a book. |
| Past Perfect | sujeto | had | [subject]'d | past participle | complemento | Subject + had + past participle + object | They had left the house. |
| Past Perfect Continuous | sujeto | had been | [subject]'d been | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + had been + verb-ing + object | I had been studying for hours. |

## 2) Negative form

| Tiempo verbal | Sujeto | Verbo auxiliar | Forma acortada del auxiliar | Verbo principal | Objeto / complemento | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Past Simple | sujeto | did not | didn't | base verb | complemento | Subject + did not + verb + object | We did not visit her. |
| Past Continuous | sujeto | was not / were not | wasn't / weren't | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + was/were not + verb-ing + object | She was not reading. |
| Past Perfect | sujeto | had not | hadn't | past participle | complemento | Subject + had not + past participle + object | They had not left yet. |
| Past Perfect Continuous | sujeto | had not been | hadn't been | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + had not been + verb-ing + object | I had not been studying long. |

## 3) Question form

| Tiempo verbal | Verbo auxiliar | Forma acortada del auxiliar | Sujeto | Verbo principal | Objeto / complemento | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Past Simple | Did | — | sujeto | base verb | complemento | Did + subject + verb + object? | Did you visit your friend? |
| Past Continuous | Was / Were | — | sujeto | verb + ing | complemento | Was/Were + subject + verb-ing + object? | Was she reading a book? |
| Past Perfect | Had | — | sujeto | past participle | complemento | Had + subject + past participle + object? | Had they left before noon? |
| Past Perfect Continuous | Had | — | sujeto | been + verb + ing | complemento | Had + subject + been + verb-ing + object? | Had you been studying long? |

---

## Quick memory

- **Past Simple** → finished past action.
- **Past Continuous** → action in progress at a past moment.
- **Past Perfect** → one past action before another past action.
- **Past Perfect Continuous** → duration before a past moment.
