# Future tenses: affirmative, negative and question forms

## 1) Affirmative form

| Tiempo verbal             | Sujeto | Verbo auxiliar         | Forma acortada del auxiliar | Verbo principal | Objeto / complemento | Estructura                                     | Ejemplo                                  |
| ------------------------- | ------ | ---------------------- | --------------------------- | --------------- | -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Future Simple             | sujeto | will                   | [subject]'ll                | base verb       | complemento          | Subject + will + verb + object                 | I will call you tomorrow.                |
| Future Continuous         | sujeto | will be                | [subject]'ll be             | verb + ing      | complemento          | Subject + will be + verb-ing + object          | She will be traveling next week.         |
| Future Perfect            | sujeto | will have              | [subject]'ll have           | past participle | complemento          | Subject + will have + past participle + object | They will have finished the project.     |
| Future Perfect Continuous | sujeto | will have been         | [subject]'ll have been      | verb + ing      | complemento          | Subject + will have been + verb-ing + object   | He will have been working for ten hours. |
| Future Going To *(extra)* | sujeto | am / is / are going to | be contraction + going to   | base verb       | complemento          | Subject + be going to + verb + object          | She is going to study tonight.           |

## 3) Question form

| Tiempo verbal | Verbo auxiliar | Forma acortada del auxiliar | Sujeto | Verbo principal | Objeto / complemento | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Future Simple | Will | — | sujeto | base verb | complemento | Will + subject + verb + object? | Will you call me tomorrow? |
| Future Continuous | Will | — | sujeto | be + verb + ing | complemento | Will + subject + be + verb-ing + object? | Will she be traveling next week? |
| Future Perfect | Will | — | sujeto | have + past participle | complemento | Will + subject + have + past participle + object? | Will they have finished the project? |
| Future Perfect Continuous | Will | — | sujeto | have been + verb + ing | complemento | Will + subject + have been + verb-ing + object? | Will he have been working for hours? |
| Future Going To *(extra)* | Am / Is / Are | — | sujeto | going to + base verb | complemento | Am/Is/Are + subject + going to + verb + object? | Are you going to study tonight? |


## Quick memory

- **Future Simple** → instant decision, prediction, promise, offer.
- **Future Continuous** → action in progress at a future moment.
- **Future Perfect** → completed before a future point.
- **Future Perfect Continuous** → duration up to a future point.
- **Future Going To** → plan, intention, or future evidence from now.
