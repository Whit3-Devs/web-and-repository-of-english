# Past Perfect

## Main Use
Use it for an action that happened before another past moment or past action.

## Timeline Idea / Visual Meaning
This is the “past before the past”.

```text
PAST [earlier action] -> [later past moment] ---- NOW
```

## Structure
- Affirmative: **Subject + had + past participle**
- Negative: **Subject + had not + past participle**
- Question: **Had + subject + past participle?**

## Examples
- They had left before I arrived.
- She hadn't finished the report when the meeting started.
- Had you seen that movie before 2023?

## Common Markers / Signals
- before
- after
- by the time
- already
- when + past event

## Contrast
Compare with [[Past Simple]] when you need to show which past action happened first.

## Common Mistakes
- **When I arrived, they left already.** ❌
- **When I arrived, they had already left.** ✅

- **She had went home.** ❌
- **She had gone home.** ✅
