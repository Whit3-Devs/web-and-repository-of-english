# Past Perfect Continuous

## Main Use
Use it for duration before another point in the past.

## Timeline Idea / Visual Meaning
The focus is on how long something had been happening before a later past moment.

```text
PAST =====> [later past moment] ---- NOW
   duration before past point
```

## Structure
- Affirmative: **Subject + had been + verb-ing**
- Negative: **Subject + had not been + verb-ing**
- Question: **Had + subject + been + verb-ing?**

## Examples
- I had been studying for hours before the exam started.
- She hadn't been sleeping well before the trip.
- Had they been waiting long when the bus arrived?

## Common Markers / Signals
- for
- since
- before
- all day
- by the time

## Contrast
Compare with [[Past Perfect]] when you want duration, not just the earlier completed result.

## Common Mistakes
- **I had been study for two hours.** ❌
- **I had been studying for two hours.** ✅

- **She had working before I came.** ❌
- **She had been working before I came.** ✅
