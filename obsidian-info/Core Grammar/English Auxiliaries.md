# Auxiliares en inglés

Este archivo reúne los auxiliares más importantes para que puedas formar:
- afirmativas
- negativas
- preguntas

Te va a servir especialmente para:
- Present Simple
- Present Continuous
- Past Simple
- Past Continuous
- Present Perfect
- Future with **will**
- Future with **going to**

---

## Qué es un auxiliar

Un **auxiliar** es una palabra que ayuda a formar el tiempo verbal, la negación o la pregunta.

Ejemplos:
- **Do** you work here?
- She **is** studying.
- I **have** finished.
- We **will** call you.

---

## Tabla general de auxiliares

| Auxiliar | Uso principal | Tiempos / estructuras | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|
| do | preguntas y negativas | Present Simple | Do you work here? |
| does | preguntas y negativas con he/she/it | Present Simple | Does she live here? |
| did | preguntas y negativas | Past Simple | Did they call you? |
| am | presente del verbo be | Present Continuous / going to | I am studying. |
| is | presente del verbo be | Present Continuous / going to | She is going to travel. |
| are | presente del verbo be | Present Continuous / going to | They are working now. |
| was | pasado del verbo be | Past Continuous | He was sleeping. |
| were | pasado del verbo be | Past Continuous | We were talking. |
| have | perfectos | Present Perfect | I have finished. |
| has | perfectos con he/she/it | Present Perfect | She has arrived. |
| had | perfectos en pasado | Past Perfect | They had left. |
| will | futuro | Future with will | I will help you. |

## 1) Do / Does

Se usan en **Present Simple** para:
- preguntas
- negativas

### Estructuras

| Tipo | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| Afirmativa | Subject + verb + complement | You work here. |
| Negativa | Subject + do/does not + base verb + complement | She does not work here. |
| Pregunta | Do/Does + subject + base verb + complement? | Do you work here? |

### Recordatorio
- **do**: I / you / we / they
- **does**: he / she / it

### Formas cortas

| Forma larga | Forma corta |
|---|---|
| do not | don't |
| does not | doesn't |

### Errores comunes

| Error | Correcto |
|---|---|
| She don't like it. | She doesn't like it. |
| Does she works here? | Does she work here? |

## 3) Am / Is / Are

Se usan en:
- **Present Continuous**
- **going to**

### A) Present Continuous

| Tipo | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| Afirmativa | Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing + complement | I am studying now. |
| Negativa | Subject + am/is/are not + verb-ing + complement | She is not working. |
| Pregunta | Am/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing + complement? | Are they sleeping? |

### B) Going to

| Tipo | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| Afirmativa | Subject + am/is/are going to + base verb + complement | I am going to call her. |
| Negativa | Subject + am/is/are not going to + base verb + complement | We are not going to travel. |
| Pregunta | Am/Is/Are + subject + going to + base verb + complement? | Is he going to study? |

### Formas cortas

| Forma larga | Forma corta |
|---|---|
| I am | I'm |
| he is | he's |
| she is | she's |
| it is | it's |
| you are | you're |
| we are | we're |
| they are | they're |
| is not | isn't |
| are not | aren't |
| I am not | I'm not |

### Errores comunes

| Error | Correcto |
|---|---|
| I am go to study. | I am going to study. |
| She are working. | She is working. |

## Resumen rápido por tiempo verbal

| Tiempo verbal | Auxiliar principal |
|---|---|
| Present Simple | do / does |
| Present Continuous | am / is / are |
| Past Simple | did |
| Past Continuous | was / were |
| Present Perfect | have / has |
| Future with will | will |
| Future with going to | am / is / are + going to |

---

## Diferencias rápidas importantes

| Auxiliar | Se usa para | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| do / does | preguntas y negativas en presente simple | Do you like coffee? |
| did | preguntas y negativas en pasado simple | Did she call you? |
| am / is / are | continuo presente y going to | She is studying. |
| was / were | continuo pasado | They were working. |
| have / has | present perfect | I have finished. |
| will | futuro | We will see. |

---

## Mini recordatorio final

- Después de **do / does / did / will**, usás **verbo base**
  - Do you **work** here?
  - Did she **go**?
  - I will **call** you.

- Después de **am / is / are / was / were**, en los continuos usás **verb + ing**
  - She is **working**.
  - They were **studying**.

- Después de **have / has**, usás **past participle**
  - I have **seen** it.
