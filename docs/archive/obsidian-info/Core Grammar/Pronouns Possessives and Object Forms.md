# Pronombres en inglés: him, his, her y otros relacionados

Si estás viendo **him, his, her**, entonces conviene completar todo el grupo de pronombres y posesivos para entender bien cómo funciona cada uno.

---

## Tabla principal

| Pronombre sujeto | Pronombre objeto | Adjetivo posesivo | Pronombre posesivo | Reflexivo | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| I | me | my | mine | myself | **I** see **my** book. / That book is **mine**. |
| You | you | your | yours | yourself / yourselves | **You** have **your** phone. / That phone is **yours**. |
| He | him | his | his | himself | I called **him**. / **His** car is new. / That car is **his**. |
| She | her | her | hers | herself | I saw **her** yesterday. / **Her** bag is blue. / That bag is **hers**. |
| It | it | its | — | itself | **It** is big. / **Its** color is black. |
| We | us | our | ours | ourselves | **We** love **our** house. / That house is **ours**. |
| They | them | their | theirs | themselves | I know **them**. / **Their** teacher is nice. / The idea is **theirs**. |

---

## Qué significa cada columna

| Tipo | Función | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| Pronombre sujeto | hace la acción | **He** works. |
| Pronombre objeto | recibe la acción | I know **him**. |
| Adjetivo posesivo | va antes de un sustantivo | **His** car |
| Pronombre posesivo | reemplaza al sustantivo | The car is **his** |
| Reflexivo | vuelve sobre la misma persona | He hurt **himself** |

---

## Las palabras que preguntaste

| Palabra | Tipo | Significado aproximado | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|
| him | pronombre objeto | lo / le / a él | I helped **him**. |
| his | adjetivo posesivo o pronombre posesivo | su / suyo | **His** jacket is black. / That jacket is **his**. |
| her | pronombre objeto o adjetivo posesivo | la / le / a ella / su | I called **her**. / **Her** name is Anna. |

---

## Diferencias importantes

### He / Him / His

| Forma | Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| he | sujeto | **He** is my friend. |
| him | objeto | I called **him**. |
| his | posesivo | **His** phone is expensive. / That phone is **his**. |

### She / Her / Hers

| Forma | Uso | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|
| she | sujeto | **She** is my teacher. |
| her | objeto o adjetivo posesivo | I saw **her**. / **Her** car is red. |
| hers | pronombre posesivo | That car is **hers**. |

---

## Regla rápida

- **he / she / they / we / I / you / it** → sujeto
- **him / her / them / us / me / you / it** → objeto
- **his / her / their / our / my / your / its** → antes de un sustantivo
- **his / hers / theirs / ours / mine / yours** → reemplazan al sustantivo

---

## Ejemplos cortos para memorizar

- **He** loves **her**.
- I gave the book to **him**.
- **His** sister is kind.
- **Her** brother is tall.
- The red bag is **hers**.
- The blue car is **his**.
- **Their** house is big.
- The house is **theirs**.

---

## Truco para no confundirte

### Si la palabra va antes de un sustantivo
Usa un **adjetivo posesivo**:

- my book
- your phone
- his car
- her bag
- our house
- their teacher

### Si reemplaza al sustantivo
Usa un **pronombre posesivo**:

- The book is mine.
- The phone is yours.
- The car is his.
- The bag is hers.
- The house is ours.
- The teacher is theirs.

### Si recibe la acción
Usa un **pronombre objeto**:

- I called him.
- I saw her.
- She helped me.
- They invited us.

---

## Resumen final

| Tipo | Ejemplos |
|---|---|
| sujeto | I, you, he, she, it, we, they |
| objeto | me, you, him, her, it, us, them |
| adjetivo posesivo | my, your, his, her, its, our, their |
| pronombre posesivo | mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs |
| reflexivo | myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, themselves |
