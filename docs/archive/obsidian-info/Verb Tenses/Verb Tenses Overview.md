# Verb Tenses Overview

This section is your **visual map** for the 12 main English verb tenses.

- Open the visual canvas here: [[Verb Tenses Timeline]]

The core system is organized by:
- **Present** = ahora, rutina, o conexión con ahora
- **Past** = pasado terminado o anterior a otro punto pasado
- **Future** = proyección hacia adelante desde ahora

And then there is one extra future form with very high practical value:
- [[Future/Future Going To|Future Going To]]

---

# 1) The 12 core tenses

## Present family

```text
PRESENT
PAST -------- NOW -------- FUTURE
Simple              | routine / fact / repeated action
Continuous          | action in progress now
Perfect             | past action connected to now
Perfect Continuous  | duration from past until now
```

- [[Present/Present Simple|Present Simple]]
- [[Present/Present Continuous|Present Continuous]]
- [[Present/Present Perfect|Present Perfect]]
- [[Present/Present Perfect Continuous|Present Perfect Continuous]]

## Past family

```text
PAST
[earlier] ---- [past point] -------- NOW -------- FUTURE
Simple              | finished past action
Continuous          | action in progress at a past moment
Perfect             | past before another past
Perfect Continuous  | duration before a past moment
```

- [[Past/Past Simple|Past Simple]]
- [[Past/Past Continuous|Past Continuous]]
- [[Past/Past Perfect|Past Perfect]]
- [[Past/Past Perfect Continuous|Past Perfect Continuous]]

## Future family

```text
FUTURE
PAST -------- NOW -------- [future point] -------->
Simple              | decision / prediction / offer
Continuous          | action in progress at a future moment
Perfect             | completed before a future point
Perfect Continuous  | duration up to a future point
```

- [[Future/Future Simple|Future Simple]]
- [[Future/Future Continuous|Future Continuous]]
- [[Future/Future Perfect|Future Perfect]]
- [[Future/Future Perfect Continuous|Future Perfect Continuous]]

# 3) Important extra future form

## Future Going To

```text
PAST -------- NOW --------> FUTURE
        plan / intention / evidence now
```

Use [[Future/Future Going To|Future Going To]] when:
- you already have a plan
- you already have an intention
- the present evidence makes the future result feel obvious

Quick contrast:
- [[Future/Future Simple|Future Simple]] → decision now / offer / neutral prediction
- [[Future/Future Going To|Future Going To]] → plan / intention / evidence now

# 4) Fast selection guide

If you want to express...

- routine or fact → [[Present/Present Simple|Present Simple]]
- action happening now → [[Present/Present Continuous|Present Continuous]]
- past experience with present connection → [[Present/Present Perfect|Present Perfect]]
- duration until now → [[Present/Present Perfect Continuous|Present Perfect Continuous]]
- finished past event → [[Past/Past Simple|Past Simple]]
- action in progress at a past moment → [[Past/Past Continuous|Past Continuous]]
- one past action before another → [[Past/Past Perfect|Past Perfect]]
- duration before a past moment → [[Past/Past Perfect Continuous|Past Perfect Continuous]]
- instant decision or prediction → [[Future/Future Simple|Future Simple]]
- action in progress at a future moment → [[Future/Future Continuous|Future Continuous]]
- completed before a future point → [[Future/Future Perfect|Future Perfect]]
- duration up to a future point → [[Future/Future Perfect Continuous|Future Perfect Continuous]]
- future plan or intention already in mind → [[Future/Future Going To|Future Going To]]
