# Past Simple

## Main Use
Use it for finished actions in the past.

## Timeline Idea / Visual Meaning
The action is complete and separated from now.

```text
PAST [finished action] ---- NOW ---- FUTURE
```

## Structure
- Affirmative: **Subject + past form**
- Negative: **Subject + did not + base verb**
- Question: **Did + subject + base verb?**

## Examples
- We visited our grandmother last weekend.
- I didn't call him yesterday.
- Did she finish the report?

## Common Markers / Signals
- yesterday
- last night
- last week
- in 2024
- two days ago

## Contrast
Compare with [[Present Perfect]] when you want to separate finished past time from present connection.

## Common Mistakes
- **Did you went?** ❌
- **Did you go?** ✅

- **I have seen her yesterday.** ❌
- **I saw her yesterday.** ✅
