# Past Continuous

## Main Use
Use it for an action that was in progress at a specific moment in the past.

## Timeline Idea / Visual Meaning
The action was happening around a past moment.

```text
PAST ---- [in progress] ---- NOW ---- FUTURE
```

## Structure
- Affirmative: **Subject + was/were + verb-ing**
- Negative: **Subject + was/were not + verb-ing**
- Question: **Was/Were + subject + verb-ing?**

## Examples
- She was reading at 8 p.m.
- They weren't paying attention.
- Were you sleeping when I called?

## Common Markers / Signals
- at 8 p.m.
- while
- when
- all evening
- at that moment

## Contrast
Use it with [[Past Simple]] when you want to combine background action and finished action.

## Common Mistakes
- **She was read a book.** ❌
- **She was reading a book.** ✅

- **They were study when I arrived.** ❌
- **They were studying when I arrived.** ✅
