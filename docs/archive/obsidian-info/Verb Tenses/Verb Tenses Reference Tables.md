# Tiempos verbales en inglés: afirmativo, negativo y pregunta

This note gives you a table-first reference for the tense system.

## 1) Tiempos verbales en forma afirmativa

| Tiempo verbal | Sujeto | Verbo auxiliar | Forma acortada del auxiliar | Verbo principal | Objeto / complemento | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Present Simple | I / You / We / They / He / She / It | — | — | base verb / base + s | complemento | Subject + verb + object | She works in an office. |
| Present Continuous | sujeto | am / is / are | I'm / he's / she's / it's / you're / we're / they're | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing + object | They are studying English. |
| Present Perfect | sujeto | have / has | I've / you've / we've / they've / he's / she's / it's | past participle | complemento | Subject + have/has + past participle + object | I have finished my homework. |
| Present Perfect Continuous | sujeto | have / has been | I've been / you've been / we've been / they've been / he's been / she's been / it's been | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + have/has been + verb-ing + object | He has been working all day. |
| Past Simple | sujeto | — | — | past form | complemento | Subject + verb in past + object | We visited our grandmother. |
| Past Continuous | sujeto | was / were | I was / he was / she was / it was / you were / we were / they were | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + was/were + verb-ing + object | She was reading a book. |
| Past Perfect | sujeto | had | I'd / you'd / he'd / she'd / it'd / we'd / they'd | past participle | complemento | Subject + had + past participle + object | They had left the house. |
| Past Perfect Continuous | sujeto | had been | I'd been / you'd been / he'd been / she'd been / it'd been / we'd been / they'd been | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + had been + verb-ing + object | I had been studying for hours. |
| Future Simple | sujeto | will | I'll / you'll / he'll / she'll / it'll / we'll / they'll | base verb | complemento | Subject + will + verb + object | I will call you tomorrow. |
| Future Continuous | sujeto | will be | I'll be / you'll be / he'll be / she'll be / it'll be / we'll be / they'll be | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + will be + verb-ing + object | She will be traveling next week. |
| Future Perfect | sujeto | will have | I'll have / you'll have / he'll have / she'll have / it'll have / we'll have / they'll have | past participle | complemento | Subject + will have + past participle + object | They will have finished the project. |
| Future Perfect Continuous | sujeto | will have been | I'll have been / you'll have been / he'll have been / she'll have been / it'll have been / we'll have been / they'll have been | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + will have been + verb-ing + object | He will have been working for ten hours. |
| Future Going To *(extra)* | sujeto | am / is / are going to | I'm going to / she's going to / they're going to | base verb | complemento | Subject + be going to + verb + object | She is going to study tonight. |

| Present Continuous | sujeto | am not / is not / are not | I'm not / isn't / aren't | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + am/is/are not + verb-ing + object | They are not studying now. |

| Present Perfect Continuous | sujeto | have not / has not been | haven't been / hasn't been | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + have/has not been + verb-ing + object | He has not been working today. |

| Past Continuous | sujeto | was not / were not | wasn't / weren't | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + was/were not + verb-ing + object | She was not reading. |
| Past Perfect | sujeto | had not | hadn't | past participle | complemento | Subject + had not + past participle + object | They had not left yet. |
| Past Perfect Continuous | sujeto | had not been | hadn't been | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + had not been + verb-ing + object | I had not been studying long. |

| Future Perfect Continuous | sujeto | will not have been | won't have been | verb + ing | complemento | Subject + will not have been + verb-ing + object | He will not have been working long. |
| Future Going To *(extra)* | sujeto | am not / is not / are not going to | I'm not going to / isn't going to / aren't going to | base verb | complemento | Subject + be not going to + verb + object | She is not going to travel. |

## 3) Tiempos verbales en forma de pregunta

| Tiempo verbal | Verbo auxiliar | Forma acortada del auxiliar | Sujeto | Verbo principal | Objeto / complemento | Estructura | Ejemplo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Present Simple | Do / Does | — | sujeto | base verb | complemento | Do/Does + subject + verb + object? | Does she work here? |
| Present Continuous | Am / Is / Are | — | sujeto | verb + ing | complemento | Am/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing + object? | Are they studying English? |
| Present Perfect | Have / Has | — | sujeto | past participle | complemento | Have/Has + subject + past participle + object? | Have you finished your homework? |
| Present Perfect Continuous | Have / Has | — | sujeto | been + verb + ing | complemento | Have/Has + subject + been + verb-ing + object? | Has he been working all day? |
| Past Simple | Did | — | sujeto | base verb | complemento | Did + subject + verb + object? | Did you visit your friend? |
| Past Continuous | Was / Were | — | sujeto | verb + ing | complemento | Was/Were + subject + verb-ing + object? | Was she reading a book? |
| Past Perfect | Had | — | sujeto | past participle | complemento | Had + subject + past participle + object? | Had they left before noon? |
| Past Perfect Continuous | Had | — | sujeto | been + verb + ing | complemento | Had + subject + been + verb-ing + object? | Had you been studying long? |
| Future Simple | Will | — | sujeto | base verb | complemento | Will + subject + verb + object? | Will you call me tomorrow? |
| Future Continuous | Will | — | sujeto | be + verb + ing | complemento | Will + subject + be + verb-ing + object? | Will she be traveling next week? |
| Future Perfect | Will | — | sujeto | have + past participle | complemento | Will + subject + have + past participle + object? | Will they have finished the project? |
| Future Perfect Continuous | Will | — | sujeto | have been + verb + ing | complemento | Will + subject + have been + verb-ing + object? | Will he have been working for hours? |
| Future Going To *(extra)* | Am / Is / Are | — | sujeto | going to + base verb | complemento | Am/Is/Are + subject + going to + verb + object? | Are you going to study tonight? |

## Observación importante

En inglés, muchas veces necesitás un **auxiliar**.

La fórmula general más útil es:

**Subject + auxiliary (if needed) + main verb + object / complement**

Por ejemplo:
- **I work every day.**
- **I am working now.**
- **I have finished the task.**
- **I will call you tomorrow.**
