# Present Continuous

## Main Use
Use it for actions happening now or around the current moment, and for temporary situations.

## Timeline Idea / Visual Meaning
Think of it as an action in progress around **now**.

```text
PAST ------- [ NOW ] ------- FUTURE
             action in progress
```

## Structure
- Affirmative: **Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing**
- Negative: **Subject + am/is/are not + verb-ing**
- Question: **Am/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?**

## Examples
- They are studying English right now.
- She isn't working this week.
- Are you listening to me?

## Common Markers / Signals
- now
- right now
- at the moment
- today
- this week

## Contrast
Compare with [[Present Simple]] when you want to separate routine from what is happening now.

## Common Mistakes
- **She studying now.** ❌
- **She is studying now.** ✅

- **I am work now.** ❌
- **I am working now.** ✅
