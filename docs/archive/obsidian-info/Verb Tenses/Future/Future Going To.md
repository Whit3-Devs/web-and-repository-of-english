# Future Going To

## Main Use
Use it for plans, intentions, and future actions you already have in mind, or for predictions based on present evidence.

## Timeline Idea / Visual Meaning
The future action comes from a present plan or present evidence.

```text
PAST ---- NOW ----> FUTURE
        plan / intention / evidence now
```

## Structure
- Affirmative: **Subject + am/is/are going to + base verb**
- Negative: **Subject + am/is/are not going to + base verb**
- Question: **Am/Is/Are + subject + going to + base verb?**

## Examples
- She is going to study tonight.
- They aren't going to travel this week.
- Is he going to call you later?

## Common Markers / Signals
- tonight
- tomorrow
- next weekend
- look at those clouds
- I have a plan

## Contrast
Compare with [[Future Simple]] when you want to separate plan / intention from instant decision or neutral prediction.

## Common Mistakes
- **I am go to study.** ❌
- **I am going to study.** ✅

- **She going to travel tomorrow.** ❌
- **She is going to travel tomorrow.** ✅
